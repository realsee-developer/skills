# Code of Conduct

[English](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) | [简体中文](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.zh-CN.md)

This project expects respectful, constructive collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and professional language.
- Focus feedback on the work and its impact.
- Respect different experience levels and working contexts.
- Keep discussions relevant to the repository.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Sharing secrets, credentials, private URLs, or account identifiers.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this code. Maintainers may also restrict participation for repeated or severe violations.

Report conduct concerns through the repository security or maintainer contact path listed in `SECURITY.md`.
